Chai, a Hindi word for tea, combines Indian spices with traditional black tea that perk up your senses and intrigue your taste buds. Spicy peppercorn, sweet cinnamon, and fresh cardamom, are just a few traditional spices that vary in each chai to create a different personality in every uniquely crafted blend.
