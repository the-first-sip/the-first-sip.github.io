--- layout: info title: White Tea description: ---

White tea offers a light, crisp flavor that leaves you feeling relaxed, rejuvenated, and healthy. Each leaf is handpicked under strict guidelines during specific growing seasons, making white tea renowned for quality. White tea has a higher concentration of essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants than green tea. The term white tea comes from the silver white fuzz on the unopened buds of the tea plant.

White tea undergoes the least amount of processing allowing the leaves to retain high concentrations of antioxidants with relatively low levels of caffeine.
